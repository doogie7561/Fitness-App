
v33 Functional Fitness Assessment

Purpose:
The functional assessment helps the app estimate the user's real-world starting capability instead of relying only on self-reported fitness level.

Assessment Tests:
1. Resting Measurements
   - Age
   - Height
   - Weight
   - Resting heart rate

2. Walking Test
   - 6-minute walk or 10-minute treadmill walk
   - Distance
   - Average heart rate if available
   - Perceived effort 1-10

3. Chair Stand Test
   - 30-second sit-to-stand reps

4. Bodyweight Squat Assessment
   - Comfortable reps
   - Pain flag
   - Form notes

5. Push-Up Assessment
   - Wall push-up
   - Incline push-up
   - Standard push-up
   - Max quality reps

6. Plank Assessment
   - Max hold time in seconds

7. Mobility Screen
   - Overhead reach
   - Toe touch
   - Deep squat comfort
   - Shoulder discomfort
   - Knee discomfort
   - Back discomfort

Calculated Scores:
- Strength Score
- Conditioning Score
- Mobility Score
- Recovery Risk
- Overall Fitness Score

Classification:
- Beginner
- Novice
- Intermediate

Personalization Rules:
- Beginner: lower starting weights, longer rest, more coaching, slower progression
- Novice: moderate progression, standard rest
- Intermediate: faster progression and higher starting volume
- Mobility limitations: suggest substitutions and reduce risky movements
- Pain flags: block automatic weight increases and recommend modifications

Reassessment:
- Every 8 weeks by default
- Optional 12-week reassessment
- User may retake anytime

Dashboard:
- Fitness Score
- Strength Score
- Conditioning Score
- Mobility Score
- Progress since last assessment
