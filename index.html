Distance examples: 0.10 = one tenth mile, 0.25 = quarter mile, 0.50 = half mile, 1.00 = one mile. For treadmill, enter the distance shown on the treadmill.

Rate only the walk/cardio test: 1–2 very easy, 3–4 easy, 5–6 moderate, 7–8 hard, 9 very hard, 10 maximum effort.

Count one rep each time you sit, stand fully upright, then sit again. Sit → stand → sit = 1 rep.
