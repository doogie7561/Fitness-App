# v35 RPG Avatar Onboarding

Added a button-based onboarding flow that combines:
- RPG starting class
- Avatar/body type selection
- Motivation selection
- Injury/limitation selection
- Functional fitness assessment
- Automatic beginner/novice/intermediate classification
- Recovery-risk guidance
- Dynamic goal-weight profile creation

Safety:
- Concerning symptoms are flagged as elevated recovery risk.
- Pain/injury selections are stored for later exercise substitution and progression protection.
- The app does not diagnose medical issues.
